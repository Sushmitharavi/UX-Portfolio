About

Hi!

I’m Sushmitha, a UX/UI Designer based in Frankfurt Germany. I have always been curious about design and psychology.
In my free time I enjoy reading books or painting (digital and acrylic). I enjoy staying indoors and snuggling up with a good movie or a good book.

My values:

Learning never ends

"Once you stop learning, you start dying" – Albert Einstein

I have always enjoyed learning new things and understanding how different disciplines work. That’s why I learnt the basics of coding to get a glance into their world of development. I wish to learn more about UI Animations and how it can shape user experience.

Passion is key

Passion is something that keeps us going even when things get slow sometimes. Reading a design book usually gets me inspired to design and to be creative.

Embrace your uniqueness

"Anything but ordinary please " – Avril Lavigne

In a world where everyone is trying to fit in, I strive to embrace my weirdness and that’s what makes me creative.

Strive to be a better version of yourself everyday

"It's no use going back to yesterday, because I was a different person then." -Alice in Wonderland.

If you really need to compare yourself to someone, compare with yourself! I think a children’s book Alice in wonderland says it all.
